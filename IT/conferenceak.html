An International Conference on Innovative Computing and Applications (ICICA’21)

ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

The primary goal of the conference is to promote current research and application developmental activities in the field of Information Technology. Another goal is to provide common platform for exchange of scientific information between researchers, developers, engineers, students, and practitioners working in and around the world. The conference will be held every year to make it an ideal platform for people to share views and experiences on recent advances in the field of Information Technology.

CONFERENCE PERSPECTIVE

There have been many computing innovations that have had, are having, and will have, profound impact on our society. Innovative computing techniques are of great help in finding effective and reliable solution for many engineering problems. The purpose of this conference is to provide a common platform to faculties, industry persons, practitioners, researchers and students for sharing knowledge, ideas and promote professional interaction between them pertaining to current state of the art in application of computing in varied domains of knowledge.

SCOPE OF THE CONFERENCE

The program of the International Conference will consist of Plenary Sessions and Technical sessions. The Plenary Sessions will be organized with an intention of familiarizing the delegates with the recent trends, best practices, the contemporary technologies and strategies that are needed in today’s business environment. These sessions will be conducted by eminent contributors in the field of Industrial Engineering. The Technical Sessions will be held where the persons from industry and academia will share their expertise, knowledge and experience so as to make a collaborative environment for knowledge sharing. The conference would be conducted and deliberated on four important tracks in the core domains of Information Technology namely Databases, Networking, Artificial Intelligence and Cloud Computing. Some of the topics identified under each domain are as follows:

  • Data Management and Quality

Networking AND SECURITY

ARTIFICIAL AND INTELLIGENCE

  • Information Retrieval and Recommendation Systems

CLOUD AND FOG COMPUTING
